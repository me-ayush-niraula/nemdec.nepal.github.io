About Us

Nepal Engineering Management and Development Consultancy (NEMDEC) is research-based consulting firm focusing on governance, public policy, planning. It imparts services for deeper impact of policy, plan and development process with due attention on social, economic and environmental issues at national and sub nation levels. Governance crisis, poor information base, weak planning practices and poor functioning of public agencies has attributed under performance of the country's development results. NEMDEC has been established in 2009 (2066 BS) as a company under Company Act of Nepal to facilitate development process and yield desire results through appreciative enquiry of approach of institutional reform and development delivery A core team of dynamic professionals having skills, expertise and experiences in the fields of governance, institutional development, engineering, management, development planning caters the needs of quality services to its clients using their networks and innovative ideas.

General Organizational Capacity

NEMDEC has gradually evolved as a multi-disciplinary research and consulting company with its innovative, technical, management and research competencies. It has delivered quality services to a diverse spectrum of organizations ranging from Governments, Development Agencies, I/NGOs and Private Sector Organizations. The company has a wide reach and spread of its services in all geographical regions, provinces and local governments of the country. It has strong legacy in the areas of research and surveys, institutional development, capacity assessment, evidence and result based planning, monitoring and evaluation, engineering, management and policy advisory services in the context of federal governance of system the country.
