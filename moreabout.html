About NEMDEC Consulting


The research-based consulting business Nepal Engineering Management and Development Consultancy (NEMDEC) focuses on planning, public policy, and governance. With adequate consideration for social, economic, and environmental concerns at the national and subnational levels, it provides services for deeper impacts of policy, plan, and development processes.
The underperformance of the nation's development outcomes has been linked to the governance crisis, a weak information basis, shoddy planning procedures, and poorly performing public institutions. In order to simplify the development process and produce desired results via appreciative inquiry of approaches to institutional transformation and development delivery, NEMDEC was created in 2009 (2066 BS) as a business under the Company Act of Nepal.
We offer world-class market and scientific research, analytics, validation, investment, and program execution services to our clients, specializing in market systems, inclusive enterprises, and biomimetic economies. We provide services to a broad spectrum of organizations, including big and small corporations, governmental agencies, and non-profits. We develop guiding visions and the tactics, interventions, and other initiatives necessary to make those goals a reality. Depending on your needs, we either directly execute these initiatives or co-deliver them with our clients or your partners. Depending on the situation and objectives, several actions may be involved in the research we perform and the treatments we devise. Species identification, systems analysis, market forecast, market linkages, business intelligence, competitions, prototyping, facilitation, workshops, conferences, farmers field schools, material production, and other real-world activities that encourage positive change for all parties involved can be included in these activities.

NEMDEC Vision

Our Vision

Through constructive, long-lasting changes in theory and practice, we transform systems for long-term success by imagining the potential of communities and businesses.

Our Mission

Creating and delivering client-centered solutions that, via creative, inclusive, and evidence-based methods, realize strategic objectives and aspirations in order to achieve returns on investments in the social, financial, and environmental spheres. Core tenets of our strategy include eradicating poverty, minimizing the consequences of climate change, including people of all genders, and doing no damage, especially in developing and frontier economies.

Our Philosophy

When it comes to producing, optimizing, and distributing resources to benefit people and the environment they live in, we think inclusive business models should be the driving force behind systemic change in the future.
